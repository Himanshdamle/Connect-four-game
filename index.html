1.

OBJECTIVE

  • Be the first player to connect four of your pieces in a row
  • Two players take turns dropping colored discs into a vertical grid

2.

WINING CONDITION

  • Horizontally [left to right]
  • Vertically [top to bottom]
  • Diagonally [both upward and downward]

3.

Gameplay

  • Players drop one piece per turn into any column.
  • The piece falls to the lowest available row in that column.
  • The game continues until a player wins or the grid is full.

4.

Draw Condition

If the grid is completely filled without a four-in-a-row connection, the game ends in a draw
